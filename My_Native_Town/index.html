About Pakistan

Pakistan is a country in South Asia. It became independent in 1947. The capital city is Islamabad while Karachi is the largest city. Pakistan has diverse landscapes including mountains, deserts, forests and coastal areas.

The country is home to the Indus Valley Civilization, one of the oldest civilizations in the world. Pakistan is famous for landmarks such as the Badshahi Mosque, Faisal Mosque and the beautiful Hunza Valley.
